About University

The University of Kragujevac, founded in 1838, is a distinguished institution in Serbia. It offers a wide array of academic disciplines and modern facilities for research and learning. Renowned for its esteemed faculty, the university fosters critical thinking and innovation among its students. Beyond academics, the campus buzzes with cultural events and sports activities, promoting a vibrant student life.

Through international collaborations, it nurtures a global perspective among its students. With its rich heritage and commitment to excellence, the University of Kragujevac continues to shape future leaders and contribute to societal progress.
